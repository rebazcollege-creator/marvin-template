§01 Biography

Between the page and the stage

Ardalan Bastani is a Kurdish writer, poet and theatre-maker from Bukan, a city in the Kurdistan region of Iran (Rojhelat). His work grows out of exile and resistance — writing and performance that carry the Kurdish voice across borders and languages.

Active in the student movement in Iran, he left the country in 2014 and reached Germany in 2015. Since 2017 he has lived in Berlin, part of the city's independent cultural and political life. He writes in Kurdish and Persian, performs in German, and translates between them.

His path runs through theatre stages, published books and the columns of Kurdish magazines — always circling the same questions: home and displacement, freedom, memory, and the worlds we carry when we leave one place for another.

Arriving as a refugee, he turned to the stage as a place to speak — building theatre out of shelters and collectives.

  1. ≈2015

    Letter TO THE WORLD

    Initiator · Actor · Translator

    A project he initiated with other refugees from his emergency shelter — shared displacement turned into performance.

  2. —
