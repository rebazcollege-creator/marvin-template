Xanî, tuned for an ADHD brain

Xanî already has the right instincts — one thing at a time, a place to dump your head, a runtime that triages the noise. These are concepts for making that promise land harder: reduce the choices, make time visible, lower the cost of starting, and never, ever shame you for a full inbox.

1 A calmer Home — one decision, warm voice

The whole screen points at a single next action. Counts are neutral, never red. Empty-ish is a feature, not a failure. Everything else is one scroll away when you ask for it.
